EGYPTIAN HOOKAHS

Are classically styled hookah pipes which are handmade in Egypt. Each hookah features a metal stem, a glass base, and a clay bowl. Traditional Egyptian hookahs are prized for their excellent performance and unique hand made details.

MODERN HOOKAHS

Pipes feature non traditional styling and designs and are generally easier to set up and maintain than a traditional handmade egyptian hookah. If you are looking for a unique base or stem design, Modern Hookahs are just the thing for you.

AL FAKHER SHISHA

Is one of the most well-known shisha brands in all the world. They have been producing shisha tobacco since 1999, and rumor has it that their influence on the hookah universe far extends that reach.

HOOKAH CHARCOAL

Quick light hookah charcoals are quick, convenient and easy to use. These coals are infused with quick-lighting compounds, making it easier for you to use your hookah pipe on the go, as they can be lit with a small lighter.
