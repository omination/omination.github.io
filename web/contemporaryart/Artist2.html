Carolyn Frischling studied at Grinnell College in Iowa, emphasizing painting, drawing and printmaking. Later, her focus on printmaking. Over time, Carolyn expanded her art practice to include 2D digital works and videos. This ultimately led her to create 3D digital images and turning them into physical sculptures.
